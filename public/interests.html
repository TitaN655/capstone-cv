Interests & Hobbies

Outside of learning and improving my software development skills with various personal projects, I enjoy photography and miniature painting. I also enjoy computer games with moderation. Beyond that I have enjoyed modifying a few computer games and in some cases I have created multiplayer levels/maps. These interests even drove me to create my own online community.

Personal Projects

  • ETernal Clan online gaming community

    In 2011 I set-up and, for years, maintained an online community where I gathered and worked with a team of people from very different backgrounds. I worked on installing, setting up, developing, and moderating a web forum built on PhpBB and multiple game servers hosted with an Ubuntu and later a Debian server. From this I learnt many useful ICT, software development, teamwork, and communication skills as well as experience of completing tasks under pressure and to promised deadlines

  • Online forum software

    My current personal project is creating an online forum software to help grow my knowledge with certain .NET and web technologies including ASP.NET MVC, Bootstrap, JavaScript/TypeScript, and SQL Server.
